Aluminium oxynitride or AlON is a ceramic composed of aluminium, oxygen and nitrogen. It is marketed under the name ALON by Surmet Corporation. AlON is optically transparent in the near-ultraviolet, visible and midwave-infrared regions of the electromagnetic spectrum.
